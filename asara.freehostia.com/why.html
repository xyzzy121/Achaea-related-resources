ACCURATE

      Areas get changed and updated more often than you think. Many map-makers aren't
      aware of this or can't/don't keep up (they make a map once and that's it). Mapping 
      is actually a continuous job, and unless updated, all other maps become obsolete. 
      Asara maps keep up with the changes and are updated quickly, real-time.

      RELIABLE

      Longevity and dependability are important: it proves usefulness and dedication. These 
      maps are convenient, easily accessible, and have been around for 6 years with a  
      consistent system. If you report a change/correction, it gets fixed. Maps are also made
      promptly, diligently, are done entirely by hand (no automation), and they always deliver.

      PRACTICAL

      These maps are straightforward with no excessive, busy artistic fluff. All lines 
      and marks contribute to the map, and none are wasted. The maps are not 
      "unwieldy": they do not require excessive scrolling or loading, and you can view 
      the entire area in a single window (or smaller) for convenient use alongside your 
      gaming client (or for printing on single pages). They are also well-detailed.

      FREE

      The maps can be used by everyone: you don't have to purchase/use ZMud, MUSH, 
      or any other specific client. You also do not require coding knowledge or time/effort 
      to figure out how to do anything. Best of all, I don't ask for your money/credits and I 
      do all the work! Who could ask for more than that?
